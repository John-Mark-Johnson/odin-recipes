Zucchini Slice

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 1 cup self-raising flour, sifted
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Beat the eggs in a large bowl until combined. Add flour and beat until smooth.
  3. Add the zucchini, onion, cheese and oil.
  4. Stir to combine.
  5. Grease and line a 9x12 pan. Pour mixture into the pan and bake for 30 minutes or until cooked.
